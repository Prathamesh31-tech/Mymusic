Khandeshi Songs :-


Khandeshi songs, rooted in
the Khandesh region of
Maharashtra,reflect the
cultural essence, traditions,
and lifestyle of the local
people. The Khandesh region,
which includes districts like
Jalgaon, Dhule, and Nandurbar,
has its own dialect of Marathi,
known as Ahirani. This dialect
gives Khandeshi songs a unique flavor and rhythm that
distinguishes them from other
regional music in Maharashtra.
